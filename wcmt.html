Best nights, best concerts

A Simple breakfast. A simple melody. When Chai Met Toast takes inspiration from the lighter side of life to create music that is heart-warming, happy and smile-worthy.
The newest band from the land of coconuts, they gained fans quickly with a special acoustic setup and a unique brand of infectious happy music,
which transcends age, genre and even language. They have performed across the country,
including festivals ranging from the most popular NH7 weekender and Red Bull Tour Bus to niche festivals like the Repertwahr Theatre and music festival in Lucknow.
Other appearances include festivals like Orange Festival, Dambuk, Annual Royal Enfield Biker Festival
Rider Mania and The Exotic Desert Festival Taalbelia.

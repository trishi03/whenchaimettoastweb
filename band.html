Meet the crew

Ashwin Gopakumar, 26, the main vocalist of band,
says, My first exposure to singing was in 2007,
when I was part of an experimental metal band Purple Blood at SCT Engineering College in Thiruvananthapuram.
Now, we have a band of my own. It is a small box of joy
with elements from different life perspectives, leading to something happy.

Sailesh being Indias only standing drummer

Francis, 27, accompany them on keyboard. Francis, who is also our producer,
add a lot to the arrangements and complete the band

The band initiated as a two-piece project by Gopakumar
and 20-year-old Achyuth Jaigopal,
one being the chai and the other the toast.
Achyuth Jaigopal plays guitar and banjos.
